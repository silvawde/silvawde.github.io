Oh Hi Beautiful...

I’m Sheena.

Let me just preface this by promising you that I will not let you sit there and mindlessly meander through mumble jumble of my skillset and experience on offer without setting the scene first. Although I do hope that you realise you really do need my offering in your life.

Firstly, I want you to know that I am forever in absolute awe of how powerful words are. The grammar, the structure, the delivery, the articulation. Luckily for you, words are what is going to help improve your business offering and get those dollar bills in your bank account. Words drive results. Copywriting is key.

Now, a little bit of background for you on how Brag Currency was born and why this business is so important to me. After working in the media and advertising industry for just shy of a decade, I have had the privilege of working with many well established, heritage global brands within the fashion, retail, lifestyle and FMCG space.

Having most recently worked as a Client Director for a global media agency working across the David Jones & Country Road Group, I have firsthand had the experience of launching multi million campaigns, planning and implementing media partnerships both big and small, being part of many brand repositioning’s, audience segmentation and strategic direction, plus a whole stack more I wont bore you with right now.

My love of writing spans way back to my childhood, so it made sense that I went on to snag myself a degree in Journalism. It is safe to say I have been writing ever since at every chance I get (it is somewhat therapy to me!)

So by combining my love of words and fashion (yep I also hold credentials in fashion business) plus my years of experience in the media & advertising world, I wanted to provide a service that specialised in helping small businesses improve their offering and ultimately drive sales. What better way to do this than in the form of copywriting?

And there you have it. That is how Brag Currency was born.

*Oh and if you’re curious about the name.. it was derived from the fact that words are acurrency you can brag with. Ta da!

Packages

Whether you are opting for a package of services or a shopping list where you can pick and choose, I begin all services with a discovery call in order to get to know the ins and outs of your business and what outcome you desire at the end of it all (don’t worry if you don’t know what you want, I will help you discover this!) from here, we will then discuss the services I offer that are best suited to the brief you have provided me with.

For every new client, it is recommended we conduct a brand personality and tone of voice analysis first, in order to provide you with copywriting direction. This analysis allows me to go deep on your brand and uncover the strategic positioning for your business, ensuring that the copy I provide you with is bespoke and true to your brand (no generic copy, I promise you).

Examples of questions I will ask you in this analysis are amongst the likes of “If your brand walked into a bar, what would they order?”, “Is your brand the type that would read Oyster magazine or RUSSH magazine?”, “What is the first thing you want your audience to know about you?”, “Who is NOT your customer?” or “Does your brand prefer to wear a beret or a bucket hat?”.

Once the analysis is conducted we get into the copy! I will provide you with a first round of copy in which you will provide feedback on, before going into the second and final round of copy. If you require additional changes this will be costed up on a case by case basis.

Finally…. the finished product! Once you are happy with where the copy has landed, I will provide you with a document that is sealed with a kiss and wrapped in a ribbon. This document entails all of the services I have provided you with so you can keep this forever and refer back to when needed. Just think of it as your very own brand copy-playbook.

Packages on offer are tiered depending on your business requirements. Package costs are charged as a lump sum rather than at a head hour rate. A 50% deposit is required upfront, with the remaining invoice being issued once you have received your completed document.
